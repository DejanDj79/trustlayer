1) Voiceover Script (Week 5, Relative to Week 4)
==================================================

Hi everyone, this is our Week 5 update for TrustLayer.

In Week 4 we focused on stability, loading polish, and reliable trend behavior.
In Week 5 we focused on intelligence depth and faster decision context.

First, we added a Risk Change Leaderboard that highlights the strongest risk movers,
with score deltas and confidence, so users can spot meaningful shifts quickly.

Second, we introduced a Liquidity versus Holder Concentration map.
This gives a fast visual triage of token risk posture and lets users click directly
into full analysis.

Third, we added narrative context with tag chips like Stablecoin, DeFi, or Meme.
Narrative is now visible in the Top Tokens table and also in the right-side detail panel.

Finally, we added shared filtering by narrative tags.
When you select a tag, both the top table and the insights section below it stay synchronized,
which keeps the view focused and coherent.

Week 5 checkpoint confirms that TrustLayer now combines scoring, movement, and context
in a cleaner analyst workflow.
