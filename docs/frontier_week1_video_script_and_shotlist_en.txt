1) Voiceover Script (Detailed, Natural, Week 1)
==================================================

Hi everyone, this is our Week 1 update for TrustLayer.

TrustLayer is a Solana token risk intelligence app that helps users quickly evaluate token trust signals before making decisions.

In this first checkpoint, we completed the core MVP ahead of schedule.
Users can paste a mint address or search by token name, run analysis, and instantly get a risk score with confidence and signal breakdown.

On the left side, we provide a live top-token market table with risk values.
From that table, users can open analysis in one click and add tokens to a favorites watchlist.

On the right side, we show detailed diagnostics:
score status, confidence level, signal details, warnings, and score history context.
This makes the result transparent instead of just showing a single number.

We also implemented watchlist monitoring with alert events.
If a watched token’s risk score changes significantly between checks, users get an alert entry.
Users can now mute or snooze alerts per token to reduce noise.

On the backend, we integrated Solana RPC with fallback logic and market data sources to keep scoring resilient even when some endpoints are slow or limited.

So by the end of Week 1, TrustLayer already supports analysis, watchlist tracking, and alert-based monitoring in one workflow.

Next week, we will focus on better explainability, stronger alert controls, and reliability hardening for final demo quality.

Thanks for watching, see you in Week 2.
