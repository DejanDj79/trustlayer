Frontier Hackathon - Week 2 Video
Project: TrustLayer
Format: Screen-only demo (no face camera)
Target length: 60-75 seconds

==================================================
1) Voiceover Script (Week 2 Final)
==================================================

Hi everyone, this is our Week 2 update for TrustLayer.

In Week 1 we delivered the core MVP.
In Week 2 we focused on explainability, watchlist alert quality, and reliability polish.

First, we expanded explainability with a "Why Score Changed" section.
Now users can see score delta, risk transition, and plain-language movement context.

Second, we improved trend analysis with 24-hour and 7-day history windows.
The graph, point count, and movement context adapt to the selected time window.

Third, we upgraded watchlist monitoring.
Users can set threshold sensitivity to 5, 10, or 15 points, review alert severities,
filter to critical-only mode, and run an immediate "Check now" refresh with visible status feedback.

We also hardened state persistence.
Watchlist items, threshold, alert feed, and mute/snooze states now survive reload reliably.

Finally, we improved UX quality:
cleaner warning readability, better watchlist layout, and smoother top-table loading behavior.

Week 2 made TrustLayer easier to trust, easier to monitor, and easier to demo.
Next, we move into Week 3 product-level polish and submission prep.

==================================================
3) Recording Checklist
==================================================

- Browser zoom 100%, full-screen app window.
- Keep one token ready with visible history points.
- Keep one watchlist token present before recording.
- Record two takes:
  - Take A: normal pace
  - Take B: slower fallback
- Keep cursor movement deliberate and avoid rapid scrolling.

==================================================
4) Editing Notes
==================================================

- Use straight cuts only (no heavy transitions).
- Keep captions short and feature-focused.
- If no critical alerts exist, show empty critical filter state and narrate as expected behavior.
- Export 1080p, 30fps.
- Keep final duration between 60 and 75 seconds.
